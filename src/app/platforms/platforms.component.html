Frameworks and methodologies supported
GHG protocol
CBP is the global environmental disclosure system or investors, companies, cities, states, and regions to manage their environmental impacts.
USEEIO
US Environmentally-Extended Input-Output (USEEIO) is a family of models designed to bridge the gap between traditional economic calculations, sustainability, and environmental decision-making.
US EPA Climate Leaders
The EPA's Center for Corporate Climate Leadership was launched in 2012 to establish norms of climate leadership by encouraging organizations with emerging climate objectives to identify and achieve cost-effective GHG emissions reductions, while helping more advanced organizations driven innovations in reducing their greenhouse gas impacts in their supply chains and beyond.
IEA National Electricity Factors
Emissions data provided by the International Energy Agency
US eGrid
The Emissions & Generation Resource Integrated Database (eGRID) is a comprehensive source of data from EPA's Clean Air Market's Division on the environmental characteristics of almost all electric power generated in the United States.
UK DEFRA
The UK government conversion factors for greenhouse gas reporting by UK and international organizations.
